Mental Health Care Accessibility Explorer

Interactive Tools for Analyzing County-Level Mental Health Care Affordability and Availability

This interactive web application provides comprehensive tools to explore mental health care accessibility across U.S. counties. Analyze affordability (AFI) and availability (AVI) metrics, examine relationships between socioeconomic variables, and model different payment scenarios to understand the financial burden of mental health care.

🗺️

Interactive Accessibility Map

Visualize county-level mental health care affordability (AFI) and availability (AVI) across the United States. Filter by state and metric to explore geographic patterns in accessibility.

 📊

Variable Relationship Explorer

Examine correlations between demographic, economic, and political variables with mental health care accessibility metrics through interactive scatter plots with trend lines.

 🧮

Affordability Scenario Calculator

Model different insurance payment scenarios (copays, deductibles, sliding scale) to understand how out-of-pocket costs vary across counties and impact affordability.

Data Source: Two Dimensions of Access: Availability and Affordability of Mental Health Care Across the U.S.
